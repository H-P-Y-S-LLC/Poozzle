BUG DOSSIERS

Know your enemies

Earwig
BOSS 01

The bathroom's warm-up tyrant. It may be the opening act, but it already knows how to turn a neat board into an awkward silence.

Spider
BOSS 02

A control freak with eight legs and zero patience. Whenever the board looks too lively, it starts redecorating with fresh trouble.

Red Worm
BOSS 03

A bad-tempered pest that likes every fight messy. If the board is not already unpleasant enough, it adds corrosion and locks down your best toys.

Sowbug
BOSS 04

A tiny armored pessimist. It curls up, changes its weakness, and rolls through your plans like your confidence owed it money.

Moth Fly
BOSS 05

A buzzing thief with the manners of a drainpipe goblin. It steals your rhythm and splashes puddles onto the board.

Mosquito
BOSS 06

A high-pressure mosquito boss that dives onto tiles and infests them with larvae, forcing you to fight control and timing at once.

Gecko
BOSS 07

A sticky-footed prowler that coats tiles with adhesive slime wherever it crawls. Every move makes the board messier.

Cockroach
BOSS 08

The roach boss never fights fair. It freezes tiles and keeps returning with fresh minions like the worst encore in bathroom history.

White Ant
BOSS 09

A white-ant foreman with a full moving crew. Hit it once, and the whole team starts hauling tiles off like a construction site.

Slug
BOSS 10

A slow-moving strategist with a moss collection and a poisonous sense of humor. It drags fights until the board stops feeling like yours.
