About This Game

Poozzle combines classic objective match-3 with milestone boss battles and data-driven long-run progression.

  • Objective-first rounds: collect colors, break blockers, and manage moves.
  • Special chains: line clears, bombs, and color clears for high-impact turns.
  • Boss battles with weakness colors and periodic pressure skills.
  • Campaign progression with irregular masks, layered blockers, and rising complexity.
  • Short-session pacing with low restart friction and steady progression.
  • Energy + rewarded refill loop built for free-to-play retention.
